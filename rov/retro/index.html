The IEEE ROV team is an innovative group of students that works each year to design and build a new Remotely Operated underwater Vehicle (ROV). The team has achieved great success at the MATE international ROV competition with multiple top 10 finishes.

Captain

Grant Geyer has had the honor of leading the team since August 2019. He joined the team in August 2017 as a member of the electrical team and has learned many skills during his time on the team. He hopes to uphold the high legacy of the captains before him.
